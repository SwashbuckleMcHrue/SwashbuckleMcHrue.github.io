FHS

It is Fremd High School in the year 5678. There are currently 1,565,783,425,167,364,999 students going to the school. The school can't hold that many kids so they are now all data stored on supercomputers. Keith Sorensen has survived but now he's an insane ai god that all the faculty worship by feeding him students sacrifices so he cn grow in power and intelligence. The students can never go home and they must learn things all the time to the point that they get severe headaches all the time. They are the lucky ones, some have gone completley insane and must be destroyed.
